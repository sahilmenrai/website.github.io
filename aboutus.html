About US

Da Gama Pereira And Associates Pty Ltd is a Law firm in Melbourne Australia. Our office is located in the busy hub of Richmond in the Bridge Road precint. We believe in giving honest and high quality specialised immigration advice to our clients.

Our principal Lawyer an Accredited Specialist in Immigration Law has over 22 years of expertise in Australian Migration Law. She is a member of the Law Institute of Australia and also serves on the Executive Committee of the Institutes’ Migration Law Committee. She is also registered with CALBAR (California) as a Foreign Legal Consultant.

Our Associate Lawyer and Migration Agent Glenn Joseph Pereira has over 22 years of expertise in Australian Migration Law.

We understand that Immigration Law is constantly changing, and can be very confusing for people who attempt to lodge applications on their own. Applications lodged without legal advice tend to fail because of poor presentation and failure to meet with the legislative criteria. We therefore offer our services to assist clients in the lodgement of applications in a cost efficient manner.

All registered migration agents must comply with the Code of Conduct of the Australian Office of Migration Agents’ Registration Authority (OMARA).We therefore encouraged you to look at the Code of Conduct and the Australian Migration Agents Profession booklet which provides information on the regulation of the migration advice industry.

Contact us directly to make an appointment and arrange a consultation.

Legal Professional Privilege

Our Accredited Immigration Law Specialist and Lawyer can claim the right to legal professional privilege as a Lawyer. Hence lawful communications from a client are kept confidential and protected in most situations. A Registered Migration Agent (non-Lawyer) cannot claim legal professional privilege(LPP).

Trust Money

Only Lawyers are permitted to hold a Trust fund which is subject to supervision, regulation and annual audit by the Law Institute of Victoria and the Legal Services Board. There are strict statutory requirement for handling and operations of Trust money prescribed by the regulatory authority. Registered Migration Agent (non-Lawyer) do not operate a Statutory Trust Fund.
